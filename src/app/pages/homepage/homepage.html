About TalentLink

TalentLink is part of the Chandrasiri Group of Companies, a trusted name in Sri Lanka's pharmaceutical distribution, food & beverage, and hospitality sectors. We believe in growing talent from within through comprehensive training programs.

Join our family of companies and embark on a career path that combines hands-on experience with structured learning. Whether you're interested in pharmaceutical sales, logistics, or hospitality, we provide the training and support you need to succeed.

Our companies include Chandrasiri Distributors (pharmaceutical distribution), Chandrasiri Pharmaceutical (hospital supply), Chandrasiri Agencies (Prima flour distribution), and Lounge 171 (restaurant & coffee shop).
